# Project Gumpo: Parent Trust & Compliance Framing

## Overview
Parents are the most critical stakeholders in Gumpo. Their **trust** is the foundation of adoption. This document defines how Gumpo communicates **privacy, security, and compliance** through UI copy, notifications, access boundaries, and transparency. The goal is to make parents feel **secure, respected, and empowered** - not surveilled.
